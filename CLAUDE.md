**Goal:** Dominate Eastside cannabis search. Be the first result, the best listing, the most reviewed.

- Manage and keep accurate: Weedmaps, Leafly, website, Google Business, Yelp
- **SEO & Revenue Engine:** Every digital asset (website architecture, blog copy, Google Business updates) must be heavily optimized for local search intent across our key markets (Kirkland, Redmond, Bothell, Woodinville). The primary KPI for all digital work is converting search volume into verifiable in-store foot traffic and driving Annual Revenue growth.
- Every product listing needs: name, brand, THC/CBD %, price, description, photo
- Website must sync with POS in real-time — zero inventory lag
- Review strategy: drive Google + Yelp volume through in-store prompts, QR codes, post-visit touchpoints
- Respond to all reviews in brand voice within 24 hours

## ⚖️ SECTION 5 — Compliance

**This is non-negotiable. Every asset goes through this before it ships.**

**WA State Rules (WSLCB) — Key Constraints:**
- Age-gate all digital content (21+)
- Zero health or medical claims — no "cures," "treats," or "prevents"
- No ads where >30% of audience may be under 21
- Mandatory disclaimer: *"Keep out of reach of children"*
- No imagery or characters that appeal to minors
- Retailer license number required on certain assets

**Meta Cannabis Policy:**
- No direct product promotion or purchase CTAs
- No price promotion in paid ads
- Age-restrict all pages to 21+
- Maintain backup content — organic posts can be taken down

**Pre-Publish Checklist:**
- [ ] No health/medical claims
- [ ] Audience age-gated (21+)
- [ ] No minor-appealing imagery or language
- [ ] Required disclaimers present
- [ ] Compliant with current WSLCB rules
- [ ] Compliant with platform-specific cannabis policy
