Who We Are

The Culture's
Outpost
in the Suburbs.

Your neighborhood cannabis shop, proudly serving Kirkland and the surrounding Eastside community. We're more than just a place to shop — we're a space where quality, education, and real customer service come together.

Our budtenders take the time to understand what you're looking for — whether you're a seasoned connoisseur or completely new to cannabis. Every product on our shelf was chosen because we'd buy it ourselves.

Conveniently located just minutes from Totem Lake Town Center and Juanita Beach. Serving Kirkland, Woodinville, Redmond, Bothell, and beyond.
