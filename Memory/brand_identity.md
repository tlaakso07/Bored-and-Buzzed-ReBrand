Phase 1 brand identity sprint completed 2026-05-06. Everything below is locked and active — apply to every asset going forward.

**Why:** Rebrand from deal-forward, generic mid-market dispensary to #1 Eastside Premium cannabis destination. Core insight from NotebookLM synthesis: "The Culture's Outpost in the Suburbs."

**How to apply:** Every asset references these tokens and voice principles. If a design decision isn't traceable to this document, it's off-brand.

---

## Positioning

- **Core insight:** "The Culture's Outpost in the Suburbs."
- **Primary tagline:** "The Eastside Standard."
- **Secondary taglines:** "Curated for the Eastside." · "Where the Eastside gets together." · "Kirkland's home for cannabis."
- **Market:** Kirkland-specific. Serves Kirkland, Redmond, Bothell, Woodinville.
- **Unlike competitors:** Lead with craft and community, not discounts.

## Brand Pillars

1. **Craft, Curated** — foundational. Product credibility comes first.
2. **The Eastside, Elevated** — positional. Owns the geographic market.
3. **Culture, In Person** — emotional. Events and community over transactions.

---

## Voice Principles

1. Be specific. Always. (Strain name + farm + specs — never "premium cannabis")
2. Cultural authority, not borrowed slang.
3. Root everything in the Eastside. Name places.
4. Let the work speak. Skip the hype.
5. Deals support the brand. Brand doesn't serve the deals.

**Retire:** "Solid weed" · "Get buzzed" · "Stay chill" · "Canna-curious" · ALL CAPS · "Elevate" · "Seamless"

---

## Logo System

- **Keep + Extend.** Existing graffiti wordmark and Bored Ape badge retained.
- **Wordmark:** White on dark (`#0D0D0B`/`#0F2640`), black on cream, gold CSS filter for special moments.
- **Badge:** Circular — never crop. Cream on dark for lifestyle. Gold ring for events/special edition.
- **Full rules:** `Brand Identity/BnB Logos/Logo-Usage-Guide.md`
