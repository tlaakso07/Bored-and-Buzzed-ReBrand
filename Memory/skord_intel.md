SKÖRD is an artisan cannabis producer and a Bored + Buzzed vendor partner. See [[MEMORY]] · [[CLAUDE]]

 This is the brand context for all co-branded content.

**Why:** B&B is doing vendor shoutout posts. Having SKÖRD's positioning locked in means copy and design stay aligned with their brand.

**How to apply:** Reference this when writing captions, designing posts, or briefing any SKÖRD content. Mirror their craft/artisan angle — they care deeply about quality, genetics, and customer experience.

## Brand Positioning
- Mission: "Change the way people think about and experience top-shelf cannabis flower and extracts."
- Philosophy: Customer experience and satisfaction over everything — from flavor to effect.
- Craft angle: Pheno-hunting, rare and exotic genetics, hand-manicuring flowers. Pride in every step.
- Drop cadence: Every 2 weeks to retail partners. Cycled to bring in new, rare genetics.
- Voice: Educational, quality-obsessed, craft-forward. Not hype — substance.
