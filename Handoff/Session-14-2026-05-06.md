## Key Decisions Made

**Logo direction: Keep + Extend.**
The graffiti wordmark and Bored Ape badge are kept in full. They're lifestyle-authentic and culturally credible. The extension is the system deployed around them: warm dark backgrounds, elevated gold, deep navy, intentional color pairings. References: Supreme, Kith, Palace — bold marks read premium through deployment context, not logo minimalism.

**Core insight:** "The Culture's Outpost in the Suburbs." — from NotebookLM synthesis of 34-page site audit + market intelligence.

**Primary tagline:** "The Eastside Standard." — claims the market explicitly, sets quality bar without stating it, works at any scale.

**Color shift:**
- `#FFD10B` → `#C9A84C` (aged gold — spirit retained, budget signaling eliminated)
- `#0274BE` → `#0F2640` (deep navy — spirit retained, corporate flatness eliminated)

**Typography:** Clash Display (display headings) + DM Sans (body/UI) + JetBrains Mono (data/prices). Montserrat retired.
