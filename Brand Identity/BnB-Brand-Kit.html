08 — Do's & Don'ts

✓

Do

  • Deploy logos on warm dark, navy, gold, or cream backgrounds only
  • Use Clash Display for all headline copy — never for body or navigation
  • Name products specifically: strain, farm, specs, price
  • Let whitespace do structural work — silence is premium
  • Use JetBrains Mono exclusively for THC%, prices, and product data
  • Reference Eastside geography: Kirkland, Juanita, Totem Lake, Redmond
  • Lead events with the badge — it's the community anchor
  • Run all copy through the voice compass: specific, editorial, warm, cultural
✕

Don't

  • No cannabis leaf imagery, generic green, or stoner visual clichés
  • No ALL CAPS body text, navigation, or deal callouts as headlines
  • No deal-first messaging above the fold — deals support the brand, not the reverse
  • No 3-column equal card grids — use asymmetric layouts
  • No pill / rounded-full buttons — 3px radius max
  • No Inter, Roboto, or Montserrat as primary typefaces
  • No pure #000000 backgrounds — use warm dark #0D0D0B
  • No neon glows, oversaturated accents, or gradient text headers
