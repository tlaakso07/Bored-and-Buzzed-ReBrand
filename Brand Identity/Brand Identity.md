## Reference Brands

70 pre-extracted design systems from world-class brands stored in `Brand Identity/Reference Brands/`. Use them as visual and tonal references during the brand sprint — tell Claude which brand's energy to match and it reads that `DESIGN.md` as context.

**Key references for B&B's premium positioning:**
- **Starbucks** — warm premium retail, community-first, loyalty system
- **Nike** — typographic authority, photography-first, kinetic brand voice
- **Apple** — restraint as luxury, product elevation, white space
- **Spotify** — dark editorial premium, culture-adjacent, lifestyle-first
- **Ferrari** — ultra-luxury positioning, ceremony through restraint
- **Linear** — dark premium digital baseline, cinematic UI
- **Airbnb** — community-rooted, warm, experiential

> [!tip]
> Blending references works well. "Starbucks warmth + Nike typographic authority" is a valid brief and produces better output than starting from scratch.
